STOP UXO

Our team built a rc tank with a electromagnetic sensor capble of detecting metal buried underneath the sand. With a 22.5 gear ratio the tank was able climb inclines up to 40 degrees. Unfortunately during the competition our 3d printed gears stripped so we didn't perform too hot.
That's okay though, because we learned a lot during this competition and did much better on the next one.
For this project I was mainly in charge of designing the drive train, creating the CAD models.
