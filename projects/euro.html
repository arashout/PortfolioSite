EURO Project

A water propelled RC car with valve control and ackermann steering geometry to prevent slippage on slippery and wet race track.
This second engineering design competition was unique because you were also graded on how well you thought your vechicle would perform through different events.
Our team did awesome, we scored most of our points in the maneuverability events and predictions.
In this project I primarily in charge of developing the MATLAB simulation used to predict our performance, building the modular chassis, developing and implementing the steering.
